Step 2: Fill Gel Box with Buffer

Filling gel box with buffer

Fill Box with Buffer Solution

Details

Now, take Beaker B - it is filled with the buffer solution that will be important for expediting the DNA solution's movement through the gel. Pour in the solution slowly, taking care not to break the gel surface. Make sure the gel is completely covered by buffer, but do not overfill. This step will not require exact measurements.
