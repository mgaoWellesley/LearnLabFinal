Step 7: Clean Up

Cleaning up

Disconnect all power Appropriately dispose of gel and buffer Wash beakers and test tubes Wipe down counters Place everything back in original position

Details

Be sure to disconnect all power sources before cleaning up, and remember to follow appropriate lab procedures. While it may be tempting to simply throw away the gel and buffer, certain solutions popular in labs can be carcinogenic and must be disposed of properly. Your instructor will provide appropriate containers to do so. Afterwards, wash all glass materials with cool water, making sure to wear gloves. Place them on a rack to dry and wipe down the surfaces you used with a mild cleaning solution.
