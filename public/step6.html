Step 6: Analyze Samples

Analyzing samples

Turn off power Remove gel from gel box Drain off excess buffer Expose gel to UV light

Details

Be certain that you have switched off the power before removing the gel (with gloves). Pour off the excess buffer into an appropriate waste disposal container. Although this simulation is mostly virtual and entirely safe, this will be an important step for you to remember in a real-world lab.
Put the gel on a clean surface, and expose the gel to UV light. This will be provided by your lab instructor. Be sure to take a picture of the gel for later use.
